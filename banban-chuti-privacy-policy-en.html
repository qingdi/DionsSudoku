Privacy Policy

Banban Chuti (斑斑出题)
Last updated: April 30, 2026

Summary: Question and configuration data are stored mainly on your device. We (the developer) do not run user accounts, upload that data to our own servers, or collect or sell personally identifiable information for our own product (such as name, phone, or student ID). To help fund the app, we use Google AdMob for app open (full-screen) ads. Ad-related processing is described in Google’s Privacy Policy and related pages. Beyond that SDK, we do not embed our own in-app analytics/profiling SDKs.

1. Data we handle

Banban Chuti generates and saves content on the device. We do not route that user content through a developer-run cloud sign-in. Locally stored items include: generation and paper settings, preferences, question parameters, local textbook options, print PDFs, and built-in templates. Content is used for app features; we do not upload it to our servers. Questions are mostly generated on the fly from parameters rather than being uploaded as a long-term “answer bank” by us.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app includes the Google Mobile Ads SDK and may show full-screen app open ads (e.g. on cold start or return from the background). Loading ads usually needs a network. Google may process technical data needed to serve ads, under Google’s terms. See Privacy Policy and How Google uses information (URLs may be updated by Google).

We do not add our own company-wide in-app “analytics/tracking for profiling” product, social login, or developer-operated K–12 cloud account for your papers. You can limit ad-related or identifier options in iOS and Android system settings as applicable.

3. Children

Adults may use the app with children. We do not require children to register with us or submit name, phone, or contact to us for a developer account. App open ads are provided by Google; any technical data involved follows Google’s disclosures. Parents and guardians can use system privacy, ads, and tracking controls (wording and placement vary by OS version).

4. Security, permissions, and deletion

Data stays in the app sandbox; uninstalling the app removes local data (back up PDFs you need). Storage, share, and print use standard OS features. We do not require location, camera, microphone, or contacts for the core product. Network and advertising identifiers may still be used by the OS, ads, or stores; limitations follow platform and Google/Apple rules.

You can delete in-app data or files as the app allows; deleting the app removes its sandbox data.

5. Changes, law, and your rights

We may update this policy in the app or on the page where it is published. Continued use after notice means you acknowledge the update, except where stricter consent is required. Where applicable, we follow laws on personal data and children’s privacy (e.g. relevant rules in China, the EU, and the U.S., subject to proper jurisdiction and conflict-of-law analysis).

You can access and remove local data on your device; other rights follow mandatory law in your region.
