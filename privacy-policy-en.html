Important Notice: We take your privacy seriously. Dinos Sudoku is a completely offline children's educational game that does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

1. Overview

Welcome to Dinos Sudoku! This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we handle information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and your child's privacy.

Dinos Sudoku is an educational Sudoku game designed specifically for children. It runs completely locally on your device, requires no internet connection, and does not collect any personal information.

2. Information Collection

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

Dinos Sudoku does NOT collect any of the following information:

  • Personal identification information (name, age, gender, etc.)
  • Contact information (email, phone number, address, etc.)
  • Device information (device ID, IP address, etc.)
  • Location information (GPS, geolocation, etc.)
  • Photos, videos, or other media files
  • Contacts or address book information
  • Usage statistics or analytics data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data

2.2 Locally Stored Data

The app only stores the following game data locally on your device. This data never leaves your device:

  • Game Progress: Current level, completed levels, star ratings
  • Game Settings: Music toggle, number/dinosaur mode, language preference
  • Game State: Saved incomplete games, game duration records
  • Dinosaur Collection: Unlocked dinosaur records

This data is only used to provide game functionality and is stored locally on the device. It is never uploaded to any server.

3. Information Use

Since we do not collect any information:

  • We do not use your information for any purpose
  • We do not share information with any third parties
  • We do not perform data analysis or user profiling
  • We do not send marketing or promotional messages

4. Third-Party Services

Dinos Sudoku does NOT use any third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • Advertising services (no ads)
  • Analytics services (no data analytics)
  • Social media integration (no social features)
  • Payment services (no in-app purchases)
  • Cloud storage services (no cloud sync)

5. Children's Privacy Protection

Dinos Sudoku is designed specifically for children. We strictly comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not collect any personal information from children under 13
  • We do not require children to provide any personal information to use the app
  • We do not include any third-party advertising or links
  • We do not include social features or chat functionality
  • The app runs completely offline with no internet connection required

6. Data Security

Although we do not collect any data, we still take measures to protect locally stored game data:

  • All game data is stored only on your device
  • We use standard system data storage mechanisms (SharedPreferences/UserDefaults)
  • Data is protected by the device's operating system security
  • All local data is automatically deleted when the app is uninstalled
